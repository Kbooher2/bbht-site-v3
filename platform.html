The Platform

The IOA Suite. End to end.

Five connected modules. One operating layer. Sold modularly or as a bundle — never as rip-and-replace. Here's what each one does.

Integrate

Module 01 · Flagship

AdmitIQ — Real-time PFR at intake.

Quote the patient accurately the moment they walk in, with live payer benefits and facility-specific reimbursement.

Integrate

Live payer feeds.

Pulls real-time benefit eligibility — coinsurance, OOP, INN/OON status — directly from payer feeds and pVerify. No more "let me call the payer."

Optimize

Facility-specific math.

Calculates estimated charges, allowables, and patient responsibility by facility — and compares projected reimbursement across your facilities so the right placement is the obvious one.

Automate

Clean handoff to billing.

Integrates with your billing system and CRM. Manages the patient financial journey via HiLite. The estimate the patient saw at intake matches the bill they get later.

Deliverables

What you get.

Eligibility checks · PFR estimates · facility comparisons · clean handoff to billing · audit trail per encounter.

Module 02

Revenue Integrity — Close the gap.

Between what was delivered, documented, and billed. Before claims go out — not three months later at close.

Integrate

Census, charges, and billing — unified.

EHR census, charge capture, and billing system feeds joined on one platform. No more spreadsheet reconciliations.

Optimize

Reconciliation by day, location, payer.

Revenue billed vs. missed — broken out where the variance actually lives. Compliance flagging built in.

Automate

Exceptions routed to the right owner.

Intelligent automation flags exceptions and routes them to the person who can fix them — not a generic queue.

Deliverables

What you get.

Census module · missed billing reports · revenue integrity dashboards · real-time net revenue monitoring.

Module 03

RCM Orchestration — A/R, denials, appeals.

Coordinated recovery across the financial workflows that actually move cash. Intelligent automation working alongside staff judgment, not replacing it.

Integrate

Sits on top of what you have.

Layers over your RCM/billing systems and clearinghouses without replacing them. Works with Waystar, Change Healthcare, and direct payer feeds.

Optimize

Collections, A/R, productivity, EBITDA.

Measured and improved continuously. Underpayment recovery surfaces money you're owed but haven't collected. Staffing analytics show where work piles up.

Automate

Denial resolution + defect recovery.

Intelligent automation classifies denials, isolates auth issues, and drives recovery workflows. Patient collections workflows handled with human-in-the-loop guidance.

Deliverables

What you get.

Optimized workflows · smart guidance · underpayment recovery · staffing analytics · compliance.

Module 04

CFO Module — Forecasts the board trusts.

The accounting layer your RCM data has been missing. Accrual close, variance reporting, and rolling forecasts in one place.

Integrate

ERP joined to RCM.

Accounting and ERP feeds — NetSuite, QuickBooks, Dynamics, others — joined to RCM and billing data. One version of the numbers.

Optimize

Accrual-grade net revenue.

Budgeting, forecasting, and banking views built on accrual-grade net revenue — not cash-only proxies that mislead.

Automate

Month-end close, accelerated.

End-of-month closing procedures, accrual-based workflows, and board-ready financial decks generated from live data.

Deliverables

What you get.

Accurate net revenue · expected income · variance reporting · roll-forwards · cash/accrual accounting.
