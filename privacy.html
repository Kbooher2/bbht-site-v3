Health Data

4.1 Scope and Definition

This section of our Privacy Policy comprehensively discloses our practices regarding the access, collection, processing, and use of Health Data through our Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ. For the purposes of this Policy, "Health Data" means any information, whether provided directly by you, automatically collected, or obtained from third parties, that relates to your past, present, or future physical or mental health or condition, including but not limited to:

  • Medical history, diagnoses, treatments, medications, or symptoms;
  • Biometric or physiological data (e.g., heart rate, blood pressure, sleep patterns, activity levels, or body metrics);
  • Genetic information or data derived from genetic testing;
  • Mental health or wellness data entered through questionnaires, journals, or assessments;
  • Any other information that, alone or in combination with other data, could reasonably be used to infer or reveal your health status.

Health Data is treated as a special category of personal information and is handled with heightened safeguards in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including (but not limited to) the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (where applicable), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and any other relevant federal, state, or international regulations governing sensitive health information.

4.2 Collection and Access to Health Data

We collect or access Health Data only when it is necessary to provide the services offered by the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ, and only with your explicit consent or where otherwise permitted by law. Collection or access occurs in the following ways:

  • Directly from you: When you voluntarily input or upload Health Data into the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ (e.g., completing health forms, uploading medical documents, entering symptom trackers, or connecting wearable devices or health apps).
  • Automatically: Through your use of the Web App's and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ's features, including device sensors, application programming interfaces (APIs), or integrated third-party services that transmit Health Data with your permission (e.g., fitness trackers, electronic health record integrations, or biometric monitoring tools).
  • From third parties: From healthcare providers, wearable manufacturers, third-party health platforms, or service partners, but only when you have explicitly authorized such sharing or connection within the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ.

We do not collect Health Data without your knowledge or consent, and we never infer or derive Health Data from non-health data unless you have expressly opted in.

4.3 Purposes and Usage of Health Data

We use Health Data exclusively for the following limited, specified, and legitimate purposes:

  • To deliver, personalize, and improve the core functionality of the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ (e.g., generating personalized health insights, progress reports, recommendations, or alerts based on the data you provide).
  • To enable features you have activated, such as health tracking, goal setting, or integration with other services you authorize.
  • To analyze usage patterns (in aggregated or de-identified form where possible) to enhance the performance, security, and user experience of the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ.
  • To communicate with you about matters directly related to your use of the Health Data features (e.g., reminders or updates to your health profile), provided you have not opted out of such communications.
  • To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests from government authorities, or protect the rights, safety, and property of the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ, our users, or the public.
  • For internal research, development, or statistical analysis, but only after appropriate de-identification or aggregation that prevents identification of any individual.

We do not use Health Data for:

  • Advertising or marketing purposes;
  • Sale to third parties;
  • Any purpose unrelated to the services of the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ; or
  • Automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects without your explicit consent or an applicable legal exception.

4.4 Sharing and Disclosure of Health Data

Health Data is never sold. We disclose Health Data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • To service providers, contractors, or processors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, data analytics, or technical support) under strict contractual agreements that require them to maintain the confidentiality and security of the data and to use it solely for the purposes we specify.
  • To third-party integrations or applications that you explicitly authorize within the Web App and Mobile Apps — AdmitIQ (e.g., connecting to a fitness device or health platform).
  • When required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the vital interests of you or another person.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or reorganization, subject to the same or greater privacy protections.

Any sharing is limited to the minimum amount of data necessary and is accompanied by appropriate safeguards.

4.5 Data Storage, Security, and Retention

Health Data is stored in encrypted form using industry-standard technical and organizational measures designed to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Access is restricted on a "need-to-know" basis. We retain Health Data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above or as required by applicable law. Upon deletion or expiration of the retention period, we securely delete or irreversibly anonymize the data.
