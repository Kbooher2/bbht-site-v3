Privacy Policy

How BB HealthTech collects, uses, and protects information. Last updated May 20, 2026.

This Privacy Policy describes how BB Healthcare Technology Partners LLC ("BBHT," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit www.bbhealthtech.com or use our services. This policy applies to information collected through our website and through the BBHT platform.

Important: Where BBHT processes Protected Health Information (PHI) on behalf of a customer, that processing is governed by a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) between BBHT and the covered entity or business associate, not by this website Privacy Policy. This policy covers website-visitor information and standard business contact information only.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including when you fill out a contact or demo-request form on our website. This typically includes:

  • Name, work email address, company name, role, and facility count
  • Any free-text message you choose to send us
  • Information you share during a sales conversation, discovery call, or written exchange

We also collect limited information automatically when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL, and approximate geographic location derived from IP. This information is used for site analytics and to improve our website. We do not use this information to identify individual visitors.

2. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule discovery calls
  • Send you information about BBHT, the IOA Suite, and related services you've asked about
  • Improve our website, marketing, and product
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third-party advertisers for behavioral advertising. We do not enroll you in unrelated marketing campaigns based on a demo request.

3. How we share information

We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., email delivery, CRM, web hosting). These providers are bound by confidentiality and use the information only to deliver services to BBHT.
  • Legal and compliance recipients where required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BBHT, our customers, or others.
  • Successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Security

BBHT operates on AWS with a Business Associate Agreement covering workloads that process PHI. Encryption is applied in transit and at rest. Access to customer data is role-based and logged. We follow industry-standard practices to protect the information we collect, but no system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses a minimal set of cookies for site operation and analytics. We do not use cross-site tracking cookies. If we add analytics tooling that requires consent under applicable law, we will display a consent banner and honor your choice.

6. Your choices

You can:

  • Ask what information we hold about you and request a copy
  • Ask us to correct or delete information we hold about you
  • Opt out of marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in any email, or by emailing us

To exercise any of these rights, email Support@BBHealthTech.com. We respond within a reasonable timeframe and in line with applicable law.

7. Children

Our website and services are intended for use by businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. International visitors

BBHT is based in the United States and processes information in the United States. If you visit our website from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated on this page.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email Support@BBHealthTech.com or call 1-(800)-960-0987 (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm EST).
